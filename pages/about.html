About us


Dr. Ronjie Mar L. Malinao

Dean


The College of Information and Computing Sciences (CICS) at Marinduque State University (MARSU) is a key academic unit dedicated to providing high-quality education in the fields of information technology and computing. CICS offers a range of undergraduate programs designed to equip students with the necessary skills and knowledge in areas such as software development, network administration, database management, and cybersecurity. The college emphasizes both theoretical foundations and practical applications, preparing students to meet the demands of the rapidly evolving tech industry. With a strong focus on research, innovation, and industry collaboration, CICS aims to produce graduates who are not only technically proficient but also capable of contributing to the technological advancement of the region and the country.
