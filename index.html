Welcome to Marinduque State University


Marinduque State University is a public college in the Philippines. Established in 1952, the college has a main campus on Boac and three branch campuses. The university offers programs in Agriculture, Science, Technology, and Trades.


Campus


The university envisions itself as a leading institution in the MIMAROPA region, recognized for its academic excellence, research contributions, and community service. MARSU's mission is to provide quality education, foster research and development, and serve as a catalyst for community development and environmental stewardship. The main campus is located in Boac, Marinduque, with additional satellite campuses in other municipalities. These campuses are equipped with modern facilities to support both academic and extracurricular activities, offering a conducive environment for learning.
